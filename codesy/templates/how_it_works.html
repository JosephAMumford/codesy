  1. Add codesy to your browser.
  2. When you visit a bug, you'll see a codesy form with two questions:
    • How much will you "chip in" for someone else to fix it?
    • How much would you ask for to fix it yourself?
  3. Bid your amounts.
  4. codesy collects all bids, and monitors all bugs.
  5. When the offers on a bug add up to someone's asking price, codesy emails them.
  6. If, and when, they fix the bug, codesy emails them to claim their earnings.
  7. They may divide their earnings up between 3 entities:
    • Themself
    • The project with the bug
    • codesy

We'll combine all the offers until they match an asking price, then notify the matching coder to fix the bug and collect their earnings.

See our Frequently Asked Questions for more.
