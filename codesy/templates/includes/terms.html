How the codesy.io Application Process Works codesy.io is an Application that matches users asking for money with users offering money for fixing open source software issues. The process follows these steps: 1. When viewing an issue on an open source project, users tell codesy how much they would contribute (‘offer’) to someone fixing the issue and how much they would want to receive (‘ask’) to fix the issue themselves. 2. Users making offers acknowledge that they shall be immediately charged for payment of their offer, and this payment does not entitle the Offeror to receive any services or goods. 3. Codesy notifies users when the sum of amounts offered matches their asking amount. 4. Users fix the bug and claim a payout by providing evidence of the fix. 5.
