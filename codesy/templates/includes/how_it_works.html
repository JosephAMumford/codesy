How Does It Work

  1. Add the codesy addon to your browser.
  2. When you visit a bug, you'll see a codesy form with two questions:
    • How much can you offer for someone else to fix it?
    • How much would you ask for to fix it yourself?
  3. Bid your amounts.
  4. codesy collects all bids, and monitors all bugs.
  5. When the all the offers on a bug add up to someone's asking price, codesy emails them.
  6. When they fix the bug, they claim the payout.
  7. Codesy notifies all the bidders and they vote to accept the fix
  8. When the voter approved the fix, the developer get the payout.
